About

VoiceRound is a convenient way to manage rest between sets during workouts. No need to tap the screen with sweaty fingers. Just say a keyword and the rest timer starts itself.

Why VoiceRound

🎤

Voice Control

Start the timer with a single voice command. Works even if your hands are shaky, busy, or wet with sweat.

🔒

Your Privacy

All speech recognition happens locally on your iPhone. The microphone sends nothing to servers.

⚡

Lightning Fast

Instant recognition without delays. Say the word — timer goes.

🎯

For Serious Training

Perfect for interval training, strength training, yoga, Pilates, CrossFit, and fitness.

📱

Works Everywhere

At the gym, outdoors, at home. With or without internet.

How it works

  • Step 1: Set your keyword (e.g., "round", "stop", "rest", or anything you want)
  • Step 2: Start your workout
  • Step 3: When it's time to rest — just say the word
  • Step 4: The timer starts automatically

Key Features

  • 🎤 Voice command to start the timer
  • 🌐 Local speech recognition (works offline)
  • 🔐 Completely private — zero data online
  • 🌍 Recognizes Russian and English
  • 📝 Keyword of your choice
  • ✅ No subscriptions or hidden payments
